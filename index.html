Download Photos from a Facebook Album

Simply login with Facebook, paste the URL of a public album below and hit "Get Photos". That's it. Learn why I build this tool.

About

I wanted to download all photos of the album Happiness of Thailand from a talented Thai photographer, namely Sarawut Intarob.

There's a few browser extension like PhotoLive but I didn't want to install one just to download an album. Therefore I decided to digg into the Graph API, and figure out if it was possible to build a tool with front-end technologies only. Here it is!

Feel free to contribute on Github.

Clicking on an image will start its download.
